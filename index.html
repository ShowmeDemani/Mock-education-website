Available majors

Choose from over 125 accredited bacherlors programs

Bacherlors degree

These courses offer new students a opportunity to get their feet wet and experience the college culture. These courses offer G.E.( General Education) credits, these are credits all students will have to take and our intended for students in their first two years as freshman and sophmores.

Masters degree

After finishing your bacherlors degree students are encouraged to continue furthering their education. This not only is more appealing to potential employers, but demonstrates true mastery of ones focus. We offer asstiance programs that allow you to continue your education free of charge after initial tuition is payed.

Post Gradution

These courses offer students a challenge and are designed for students with a existing background. These courses offer more credits as a result, but do be cautious if taking these courses in Your first two years as freshman and sophmores generally need G.E.( General Education) classes in order to be prepared.
